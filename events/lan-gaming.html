Frequently Asked Questions

What games will be played at LAN Gaming?

The tournament will feature popular titles including CS:GO (5v5), Valorant (5v5), FIFA (1v1), and possibly more games based on participant interest.

Do I need to bring my own gaming setup?

No! We provide high-performance gaming PCs, monitors, keyboards, mice, and headsets. Just bring yourself and your team!

Can I participate in multiple game tournaments?

Yes! You can register and compete in multiple game tournaments as long as the match timings don't overlap.

What is the team size for each game?

CS:GO and Valorant require 5-player teams. FIFA is 1v1. Team sizes may vary for other games announced closer to the event.

How do I register for LAN Gaming?

First, purchase your Spandan pass. Then, game-specific registration will open closer to the event date. Team captains can register their full team online.
